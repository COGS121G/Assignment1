ABOUT GRANDTIME

GrandTime exists to provide a great social network for grandparents to share positive experiences with your grandchildren. We provide a senior friendly user interface so you never have to be confused about interacting again!

    HOW TO:

  • Write a message on top! Express your funny or nice times with your grandchildren
  • Search users or other messages!
  • Feel free to vote up and down on your favorate messages so others know which content is best!
